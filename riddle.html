The 100 Lockers Puzzle
Picture a school hallway with 100 closed lockers numbered 1 to 100. Now imagine 100 students lined up for a peculiar ritual...
  • The first student walks down the hall and opens every single locker
  • The second student follows, closing lockers 2, 4, 6, and so on
  • The third student goes down the hall and for every third locker, if it is closed, he opens it, if open, he closes it.
  • The pattern continues: each student n toggles every nth locker (starting with their own number)
After all students have taken their turn, how many lockers remain open?
